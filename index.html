SAMPS

Smart Air Response Assistant

Problem

Air pollution causes serious health problems, especially for asthma patients, children, and the elderly. Most air purifiers require manual operation and do not respond dynamically.

Solution

SAMPS is an intelligent air monitoring and purification system that continuously measures air quality and automatically activates purification when pollution rises.

How It Works

  • Sensors monitor air quality in real time
  • ESP32 processes AQI data
  • Purifier activates automatically
  • Manual override for user control

Key Features

  • Real-time AQI monitoring
  • Automatic & manual modes
  • Carbon + HEPA + UV filtration
  • Energy-efficient design

Technology Used

  • ESP32 microcontroller
  • MQ-series gas sensors
  • DHT22 temperature & humidity sensor
  • Fan & UV control system

Impact

SAMPS improves indoor air safety by responding instantly to pollution, reducing health risks and dependence on manual control.
