FAQ


What is SAMPS?

SAMPS is a Smart Air Monitoring & Purification System designed for intelligent indoor air management.

Does it work automatically?

Yes. The system adapts fan speed and purification level based on AQI.

Can I control it remotely?

Yes, via Blynk IoT and future web-based dashboards.

Is UV exposure safe?

Yes. UV is fully enclosed and only operates when the system is sealed.
